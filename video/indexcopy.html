John Edmond
Founding Partner, Legal and Compliance
John has worked in insurance for more than 35 years. His first job was in Essex in the policy signing office of a large UK life insurer.

John is highly regarded across all classes of insurance and reinsurance. He brings to Artisan a depth of experience in coverage, as well as having a commercial approach to quickly resolving insurance claims, and delivering our clients outstanding service.
